Financial accessibility & free testing

  • We will work with you to ensure your financial background doesn't prevent you from using our services.
  • Some people are eligible to receive free or discounted genetic testing.
  • If you live in a zip code with an average of less than $42,000 in adjusted gross income in 2020, you are eligible for free analysis.
  • If you live in a household with total combined income under $90K, you are eligible for free testing.
